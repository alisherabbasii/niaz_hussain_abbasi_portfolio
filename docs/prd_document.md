# PRD – Niaz Hussain Abbasi Landing Page (Refined)

## Positioning

A modern, high-trust personal brand website combining:

- Engineering professionalism
- Human story (resilience + social impact)

---

## Core Message

"Engineering precision with human purpose"

---

## UX Goals

- Instantly communicate credibility (within 3 seconds)
- Make contact effortless
- Balance technical + personal identity

---

## Sections (Refined)

### 1. Hero (High Impact)

- Strong headline:
  "Civil Engineer | Site Specialist | Community Voice"
- Subtext:
  "Building structures, empowering people"
- CTA:
  - Primary: Contact
  - Secondary: View Experience

---

### 2. About (Story-driven)

- Blend:
  - Professional journey
  - Overcoming challenges (subtle, empowering tone)

---

### 3. Experience (Timeline UI)

- Focus on:
  - Real-world site work
  - Harsh environments (mountain/rocky terrain)

---

### 4. Skills (Visual-first)

- Grouped:
  - Engineering Tools
  - Project Management
  - Communication

---

### 5. Work Highlights

- Emphasize:
  - Practical execution
  - Problem-solving

---

### 6. Beyond Engineering

- Poet
- Social Worker
- YouTuber
  👉 Keep visually distinct (cards)

---

### 7. Values

- Helping others
- Leadership inspiration (neutral tone)

---

### 8. Contact (Conversion-focused)

- Sticky CTA
- Minimal friction

---

## Visual Rules

- Avoid clutter
- Use whitespace aggressively
- 60/30/10 color ratio
